About

I’m a 5th‑year M.S.–Ph.D. student at Yonsei University. My work bridges battery health diagnostics with power electronics, focusing on signal injection, over‑potential modeling, and time‑frequency analysis of inverter systems.

  • IEEE TIE/TIM first‑author papers in preparation
  • Supraharmonics & PQ analytics at 100 kHz
